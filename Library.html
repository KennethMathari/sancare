    LIBRARY

    Our well-stocked library offers a wide range of fiction and non-fiction books, daily newspapers, periodicals, current journals and educational reports.

    The library is led by a strong team of highly qualified and friendly librarians who are always ready to assist the users

    The major role of the library is to encourage positive attitudes amongst the students, teachers and the rest of the staff who use the facility thus promoting a reading culture among the various groups. Its end result is to disseminate valuable information to all and in life-long learning.

    The Library provides strong support to students, teachers and researchers for their study by providing relevant current information materials. We strive to offer a vibrant collection of a variety of fiction and nonfiction books, periodicals, current journals and educational reports. For easy retrieval of these information materials the librarian classifies them using the Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme and provide a catalog for each material.

    Students come to the library each week for book exchange, featured selections and to learn basic library skills.

    We have a reading scheme that starts at the BabyClass level all through to the Primary level.
