    Curriculum

    Sancare has carved out a niche as a centre of excellence in primary education within the 8-4-4 system of Education.

    Core subjects taught at the primary school level are: English, Maths, Kiswahili, Science, Social Studies and Christian Religious studies. All these subjects are examined in the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education at the end of the 8 year course. The pupils also engage in classes in Music, Physical Education, Swimming and Art & Craft.

    We take the arts seriously at SanCare and encourage students to participate in concerts, exhibitions and competitions in their relevant fields of art and craft as well as music.

    CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

    The school has highly qualified trainers for co-curricular activities. The activities offered at Sancare Preparatory School include:

    • Learning
    • Games & Sports
    • Guidance & Counselling
    • Scouting
    • Debate
    • Life Skills
    • Spiritual awareness
