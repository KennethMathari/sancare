    Parents and Students

    1.PTA

    Through the Parents and Teachers Association, parents are able to come together and identify ways of supporting the school. The ideas they bring on board go a long way in involving them in the education of their children, and engaging the school's administration on the improvement of standards and quality education at the constituent schools.

    2.PARENTS AND TEACHERS INTERACTIVE MEETINGS

    We actively encourage Sancare parents to participate in the education of their children through regular interactive sessions with the teachers and management. During these meetings parents are given the necessary information and tools to help them support their children in academic and social development
