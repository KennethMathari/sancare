    SPORTS

    Sancare preparatory school insistence on excellence extends beyond the classroom into the sports programme. Students learn the critical importance of a lifetime of physical fitness. In addition, athletics are seen as a laboratory of leadership where students are given the opportunity to develop self-discipline and a spirit of co-operation as members of a team. The school participates in a wid range of sports. These include:

  • Swimming
  • Football
  • Athletics
