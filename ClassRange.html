    CLASS RANGE

    Sancare Preparatory School has a wide class range from pre-school level to primary level.

    Pre-school

    The Pre-school level comprises of baby class, nursery and pre-unit.Catering for children between the ages of 3 and 6 years, Sancare pre-school offers a friendly learning environment steered by well qualified teachers to provide a rich blend of academic and extracurricular activities. Our goal is to offer children a sound early childhood education, while teaching them about their rich Kenyan culture through carefully chosen class activities.

    The small classes allow qualified teachers to address the unique needs of each child. In many ways, Sancare pre-school is a home away from home, ensuring plenty of smiling faces and happy voices each day!

    Primary

    The Primary level comprises of standard one to standard eight.Catering for children between the ages of 7 and 14 years.With a keen focus on providing holistic education for our pupils, we embrace an approach that addresses all facets of growth and development. Our aim is to make every pupil the best he/she can in all areas of development. Our activities are, therefore well planned and tailor made to enable the learner attain maximum knowledge through practical participation.
