School Background

Sancare Preparatory school was founded and legally registered in 2012 by Grace Kimotho who is the school's director.The school is registered under non-formal education sector and operates in Huruma slum area of Nairobi. The school was started with the aim of assisting orphans and vulnerable children to gain access to basic education. Its other aim was to create employment for the teaching staff as well as support staff. The classes range from baby class to standard 8. The school has also prepared students sufficiently so as to sit for the K.C.P.E national examinations. The fees are highly subsidized to cater for needy students who cannot afford the high cost of learning.

Initially starting with 280 pupils, the school population has steadily grown to 600+ learners by 2014. Currently the student population consists of orphans and other children severely marginalized by poverty as well as those suffering from HIV/AIDS.

School activities:

The school strives to offer a holistic education through the following activities

  1. Learning
  2. Games and Sports
  3. Guidance and counselling
  4. Scouting
  5. Debate
  6. Life Skills
  7. Spiritual awareness

Achievements

  • The school has expanded over the years from 280 children in 2012 to 600+ in 2014.
  • The school football team were awarded ksh.20,000 after emerging winners in a sports festival comprising of over 20 schools in November 2013.
  • The debate club has become very active and won at various debating meetings with other schools.
  • The school has been able to identify and support needy children in the neighbourhood through the help of various NGO's. Hence brightening the future of the needy children and giving them hope.
